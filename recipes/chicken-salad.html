Chicken Salad

A health dish comprising of a mixed salad and grilled chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Salad leaves
  • Salad dressing
  • Feta cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Grill the chicken on both sides for 5-7 minutes
  2. Wash the salad leaves and place ina bowl
  3. Cover the salad leaves in the dressing
  4. Place the grilled chicken on top
  5. Add in the feta around the side or mix through the salad
