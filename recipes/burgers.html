Burgers

An american classic, burgers on the BBQ

Prime mince beef with a flame grill finish inside a freshly baked breadbun... What more could you ask for?

Ingredients

  • Beef Burgers
  • Bread buns
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Cheese
  • Gherkins

Steps

  1. Place the burgers onto the grill and cook through
  2. add the cheese ontop and allow to melt
  3. Put mayo on one side of the bread bun and ketchup on the other
  4. Put the burgers in the breadbun
  5. Add sliced gherkins to the top (optional)
  6. Enjoy!
